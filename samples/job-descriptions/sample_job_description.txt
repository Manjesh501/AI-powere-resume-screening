Senior Backend Engineer

Company: TechStart Inc.
Location: Remote

POSITION OVERVIEW
-----------------
We are seeking a Senior Backend Engineer to join our growing engineering team. You will be responsible for 
designing, developing, and maintaining scalable backend services that power our platform. The ideal 
candidate has extensive experience with Node.js, microservices architecture, and cloud technologies.

RESPONSIBILITIES
----------------
• Design and implement high-performance, scalable backend services using Node.js
• Develop and maintain RESTful APIs consumed by web and mobile applications
• Collaborate with frontend engineers to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic
• Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability
• Implement security and data protection measures
• Participate in code reviews and contribute to engineering best practices
• Mentor junior developers and contribute to team growth

REQUIREMENTS
------------
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience
• 5+ years of experience in backend development
• Proficiency in Node.js, Express, and related frameworks
• Experience with microservices architecture and distributed systems
• Strong knowledge of database design and management (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
• Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
• Experience with containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes)
• Understanding of RESTful APIs and web services
• Knowledge of testing frameworks and CI/CD pipelines
• Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail

NICE TO HAVE
------------
• Experience with React or other frontend frameworks
• Knowledge of message queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka)
• Familiarity with monitoring and logging tools (Prometheus, Grafana)
• Experience with GraphQL
• Understanding of machine learning concepts
